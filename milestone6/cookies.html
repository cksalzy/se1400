Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Process

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cream together sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Sift together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Mix in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 9 minutes. Cookies will be soft. Cool slightly on cookie sheet; remove from sheet onto a wire rack to cool completely.
